Basic Info About Me

Hi there kind folks! I am a spirited high school senior at Budhanilkantha School. My heart overflows with passion for the intricacies of web development, the mysteries of artificial intelligence, and the crucial field of cyber security. My insatiable curiosity and unwavering devotion to the world of computing drives me to constantly seek out new technologies to master. Presently, I am expanding my skillset by delving into the multifaceted realm of full-stack development, using the dynamic duo of React and Django, while also exploring the intriguing disciplines of AI and machine learning. Beyond the realm of programming, I find joy in solving intricate mathematical problems, tinkering with electronics, venturing to new and exotic locales, and immersing myself in the knowledge and insights contained within the pages of books.
